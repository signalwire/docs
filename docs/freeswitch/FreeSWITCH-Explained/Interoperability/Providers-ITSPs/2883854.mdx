
# Provider Teliax (USA) 

 

## [www.teliax.com](https://www.teliax.com)

Last update: 16 June 2009

Works with incoming and outgoing SIP.

conf/sip\_profiles/external/teliax.xml - You may need to change your realm to a different Teliax server other than [atl.teliax.net](http://atl.teliax.net).

```xml
<include>
     <gateway name="teliax">
        <param name="username" value="Your Username"/>
        <!-- Add in your teliax realm here, follow the instructions on the teliax website -->
        <param name="realm" value="atl.teliax.net"/>
        <param name="password" value="Your Password"/>
    </gateway>
</include>
```
