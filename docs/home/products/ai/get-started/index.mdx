# Create your first AI Agent

<Subtitle>Deploy a no-code AI Agent</Subtitle>

<Steps>

### Create a <a href="https://id.signalwire.com/onboarding" target="_blank">new SignalWire account</a>

To begin, sign up for a SignalWire account. If you already have an account, log in.

Once logged in, create a Space or select an existing Space.

### Create an AI Agent Resource

Open the <a href="https://my.signalwire.com?page=resources" target="_blank">Resources</a> tab in your SignalWire Space.

Click <kbd>+ Add New</kbd> to create a new Resource. 
Select "AI Agent" from the menu.
If prompted, choose **Custom AI Agent**.

![Resources selection.](/docs/_common/img/dashboard/resources/add-new-resource.webp)

<Admonition type="info" title="What's a Resource?">Resources are the building blocks of every SignalWire communication application. <a href="/platform/call-fabric/managing-your-signalwire-resources#resource-types">Resources</a> include AI Agents, Subscribers, RELAY applications, FreeSWITCH connectors, and more.</Admonition>

### Fund your account

[Add funds][2] to your SignalWire Space in order to purchase a phone number and initialize your AI Agent.

Alternatively, [contact Support][1] to add a one-time Trial credit to your Space.

### Assign a phone number

[Buy a phone number][3].

### Give it a call

Dial your newly configured AI Agent over the PSTN from your cell phone or a VoIP dialer.

</Steps>
