# Create your first AI Agent

<Subtitle>Deploy a serverless AI Agent and call it over the PSTN in under 5 minutes - **for free**</Subtitle>

<Steps>

### Create a <a href="https://id.signalwire.com/onboarding" target="_blank">new SignalWire account</a>

To begin, sign up for a SignalWire account. 
If you already have an account, log in.
Create a Space or select an existing Space.

### Open the <a href="https://my.signalwire.com?page=resources" target="_blank">Resources</a> tab in your SignalWire Space

<div class="row">
  <div class="col col--6">
    Open the [Relay / SWML](https://my.signalwire.com?page=relay) tab in your SignalWire Dashboard,
    paste the following script, and hit <kbd>Save</kbd>.

    <Admonition type="info" title="Fun fact">This simple YAML/JSON document is a complete calling application!</Admonition>
  </div>
  <div class="col col--6">
  ```yaml title="swml.yaml" showLineNumbers
  version: 1.0.0
  sections:
    main:
      - ai:
          prompt:
            text: You are a knowledgeable developer. Have an open-ended discussion with the caller about SignalWire and programmable communications.
  ```
  </div>
</div>

### Assign a phone number

[Buy a phone number](https://my.signalwire.com?page=relay) using your $5 promotional credit.

### Give it a call

Dial your newly configured AI Agent over the PSTN from your cell phone or a VoIP dialer.

</Steps>
