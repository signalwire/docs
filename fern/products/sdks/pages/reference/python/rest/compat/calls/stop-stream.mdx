## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="call_sid" type="str" required={true} toc={true}>

</ParamField>

<ParamField path="stream_sid" type="str" required={true} toc={true}>

</ParamField>

`dict` -- The updated stream resource.

client = RestClient()
