Remove a participant from a conference, ending their call leg.

## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="conference_sid" type="str" required={true} toc={true}>
  The SID of the conference.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="call_sid" type="str" required={true} toc={true}>
  The call SID of the participant to remove.
</ParamField>

## **Returns**

`dict` -- Empty dict on success.

## **Example**

```python
from signalwire.rest import RestClient

client = RestClient()
client.compat.conferences.remove_participant("CF...", "CA...")
```
