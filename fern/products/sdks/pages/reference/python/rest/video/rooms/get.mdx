Retrieve a single video room by its unique identifier.

## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="resource_id" type="str" required={true} toc={true}>
  The unique identifier of the video room.
</ParamField>

## **Returns**

`dict` -- The video room resource.

## **Example**

```python
from signalwire.rest import RestClient

client = RestClient()
room = client.video.rooms.get("room-id")
```
