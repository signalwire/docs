Retrieve a single room recording by its unique identifier.

## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="recording_id" type="String" required={true} toc={true}>
  The unique identifier of the recording.
</ParamField>

## **Returns**

`Hash` -- The room recording resource, including download URL and metadata.

## **Example**

```ruby {9-9}
require 'signalwire/rest/rest_client'

client = SignalWire::REST::RestClient.new(
    project="your-project-id",
    token="your-api-token",
    host="your-space.signalwire.com",
)

recording = client.video.room_recordings["recording-id"]
```
