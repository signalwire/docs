Retrieve a specific stream by its unique identifier, regardless of which room
or conference it belongs to.

## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="stream_id" type="String" required={true} toc={true}>
  The unique identifier of the stream.
</ParamField>

## **Returns**

`Hash` -- The stream resource.

## **Example**

```ruby {9-9}
require 'signalwire/rest/rest_client'

client = SignalWire::REST::RestClient.new(
    project="your-project-id",
    token="your-api-token",
    host="your-space.signalwire.com",
)

stream = client.video.streams["stream-id"]
```
