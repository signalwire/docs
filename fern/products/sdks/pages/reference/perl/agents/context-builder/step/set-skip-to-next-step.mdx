[ref-step]: /docs/sdks/reference/perl/agents/context-builder/step

Automatically advance to the next step without evaluating step criteria. Useful
for steps that should always flow through sequentially.

## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="skip" type="$boolean" required={true} toc={true}>
  Whether to skip to the next step automatically.
</ParamField>

## **Returns**

[`Step`][ref-step] -- Self for method chaining.

## **Example**

```perl {9}
use SignalWire::Agent::AgentBase;

my $agent = SignalWire::Agent::AgentBase->new(name => "my-agent", route => "/agent");

my $contexts = $agent->define_contexts();
my $ctx = contexts.add_context("default");
my $preamble = $ctx->add_step("preamble");
preamble.set_text("Read the disclaimer to the caller.");
preamble.set_skip_to_next_step(1);
$ctx->add_step("main")->set_text("Help the caller with their request.");

$agent->serve();
```
