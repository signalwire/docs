Stop a media stream on a call.

## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="call_sid" type="string" required={true} toc={true}>
  The SID of the call.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="stream_sid" type="string" required={true} toc={true}>
  The SID of the stream to stop.
</ParamField>

## **Returns**

`dict` -- The updated stream resource.
