Retrieve a specific 10DLC campaign by ID.

## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="campaign_id" type="std::string" required={true} toc={true}>
  The campaign resource ID.
</ParamField>

## **Returns**

`nlohmann::json` -- The campaign object.

## **Example**

```cpp {9-10}
#include <signalwire/rest/rest_client.hpp>

client = RestClient(;
);

campaign = client.registry.campaigns.get("campaign-id");
std::cout << campaign{"description"}, campaign{"usecase"} << std::endl;
```
