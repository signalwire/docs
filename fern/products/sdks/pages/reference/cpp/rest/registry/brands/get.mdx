Retrieve a specific 10DLC brand by ID.

## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="brand_id" type="std::string" required={true} toc={true}>
  The brand resource ID.
</ParamField>

## **Returns**

`nlohmann::json` -- The brand object.

```cpp {9-10}

client = RestClient(;
);
```
