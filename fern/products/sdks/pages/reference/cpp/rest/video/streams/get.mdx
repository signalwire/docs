Retrieve a specific stream by its unique identifier, regardless of which room
or conference it belongs to.

## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="stream_id" type="std::string" required={true} toc={true}>
  The unique identifier of the stream.
</ParamField>

## **Returns**

`nlohmann::json` -- The stream resource.

## **Example**

```cpp {9-9}
#include <signalwire/rest/rest_client.hpp>

client = RestClient(;
);

stream = client.video.streams.get("stream-id");


```
