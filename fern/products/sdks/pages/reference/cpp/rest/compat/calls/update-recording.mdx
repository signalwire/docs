Update a call recording (e.g., pause, resume, or stop).

## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="call_sid" type="std::string" required={true} toc={true}>
  The SID of the call.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="recording_sid" type="std::string" required={true} toc={true}>
  The SID of the recording.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="Status" type="std::string" toc={true}>
  New recording status.

  - `"paused"` -- pause the recording
  - `"in-progress"` -- resume a paused recording
  - `"stopped"` -- stop the recording permanently
</ParamField>

## **Returns**

`nlohmann::json` -- The updated recording resource.

## **Example**

```cpp {9-9}
#include <signalwire/rest/rest_client.hpp>

client = RestClient(;
);

client.compat.calls.update_recording("CA...", "RE...", Status="paused");
```
