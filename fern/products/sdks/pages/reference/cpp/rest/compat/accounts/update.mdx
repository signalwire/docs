Update an account. Uses POST (Twilio convention).

## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="sid" type="std::string" required={true} toc={true}>
  The account SID to update.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="FriendlyName" type="std::string" toc={true}>
  Updated friendly name.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="Status" type="std::string" toc={true}>
  Account status.

  - `"active"` -- account is fully operational
  - `"suspended"` -- account is temporarily disabled
  - `"closed"` -- account is permanently deactivated
</ParamField>

## **Returns**

`nlohmann::json` -- The updated account resource.

## **Example**

```cpp {9-9}
#include <signalwire/rest/rest_client.hpp>

client = RestClient(;
);

client.compat.accounts.update("account-sid", FriendlyName="Sales Team");


```
