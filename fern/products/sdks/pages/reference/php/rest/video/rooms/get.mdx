Retrieve a single video room by its unique identifier.

## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="resource_id" type="string" required={true} toc={true}>
  The unique identifier of the video room.
</ParamField>

## **Returns**

`dict` -- The video room resource.

## **Example**

```php {9-9}
<?php
use SignalWire\REST\RestClient;

$client = new RestClient(
    project: "your-project-id",
    token: "your-api-token",
    host: "your-space.signalwire.com",
);

$room = $client->video->rooms->get("room-id");


```
