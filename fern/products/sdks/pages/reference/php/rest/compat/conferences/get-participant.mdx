Retrieve a specific participant in a conference.

## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="conference_sid" type="string" required={true} toc={true}>
  The SID of the conference.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="call_sid" type="string" required={true} toc={true}>
  The call SID of the participant.
</ParamField>

## **Returns**

`dict` -- The participant resource.

## **Example**

```php {9-9}
<?php
use SignalWire\REST\RestClient;

$client = new RestClient(
    project: "your-project-id",
    token: "your-api-token",
    host: "your-space.signalwire.com",
);

$participant = $client->compat->conferences->get_participant("CF...", "CA...");
```
