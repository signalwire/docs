```ts
deviceInfoToConstraints(deviceInfo): MediaTrackConstraints
```

## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="deviceInfo" type="MediaDeviceInfo | null" required toc={true}>
  Device descriptor to convert, or `null` for unconstrained capture.
</ParamField>

## **Returns**

`MediaTrackConstraints`
