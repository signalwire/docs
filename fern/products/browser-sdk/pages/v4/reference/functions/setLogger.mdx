```ts
setLogger(logger): void
```
Replace the built-in logger with a custom implementation. Pass `null` to restore defaults.

## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="logger" type="SDKLogger | null" toc={true}>
  Custom logger implementation, or `null` to restore the default.
</ParamField>

## **Returns**

`void`
