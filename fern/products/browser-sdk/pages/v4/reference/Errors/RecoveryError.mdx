Error thrown when a recovery attempt fails.

Carries the recovery action and attempt number for diagnostic purposes.

## **Extends**

- `Error`

## **Constructors**

### Constructor

```ts
new RecoveryError(action, attempt, originalError?): RecoveryError
```

[Go to Code](https://github.com/signalwire/browser-sdk/blob/main/packages/main/src/core/errors.ts#L421)

### Parameters

<ParamField path="action" type="string" required toc={true}>
  Recovery action being attempted when the error was raised.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="attempt" type="number" required toc={true}>
  Recovery attempt number (1-based) at the time of failure.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="originalError" type="unknown" toc={true}>
  Underlying error that caused the recovery attempt to fail.
</ParamField>
