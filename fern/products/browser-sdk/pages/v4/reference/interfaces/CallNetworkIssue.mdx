## **Properties**

<ParamField path="severity" type="warning | critical" required toc={true}>
  Severity of the issue. `warning` indicates degradation; `critical` indicates the call may not be usable.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="threshold" type="number" toc={true}>
  Threshold value (in the metric's unit) that was crossed to trigger this issue.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="timestamp" type="number" required toc={true}>
  Wall-clock timestamp (ms since epoch) when the issue was detected.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="type" type="no_inbound_audio | no_inbound_video | high_rtt | high_packet_loss | high_jitter | ice_disconnected" required toc={true}>
  Kind of network issue detected.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="value" type="number" toc={true}>
  Observed value of the metric that triggered the issue.
</ParamField>
