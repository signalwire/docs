Call-level capabilities state

## **Properties**

<ParamField path="device" type="boolean" required toc={true}>
  Whether the local participant can change devices on the call.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="end" type="boolean" required toc={true}>
  Whether the local participant can end the call.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="lock" type="OnOffCapability" required toc={true}>
  Capability to lock or unlock the call.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="member" type="MemberCapabilities" required toc={true}>
  Capabilities applied to other members on the call.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="screenshare" type="boolean" required toc={true}>
  Whether screen-sharing is allowed.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="self" type="MemberCapabilities" required toc={true}>
  Capabilities applied to the local participant.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="sendDigit" type="boolean" required toc={true}>
  Whether DTMF digits can be sent on this call.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="setLayout" type="boolean" required toc={true}>
  Whether the layout can be changed.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="vmutedHide" type="OnOffCapability" required toc={true}>
  Capability to hide the video tile when video is muted.
</ParamField>
