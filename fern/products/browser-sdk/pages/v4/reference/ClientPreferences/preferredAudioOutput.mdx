Preferred audio output device for new calls.

## **Parameters**

<ParamField path="value" type="MediaDeviceInfo | null" toc={true}>
  Preferred audio output device, or `null` to clear. See [`MediaDeviceInfo`](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/API/MediaDeviceInfo).
</ParamField>

## **Examples**

```ts
console.log(client.preferences.preferredAudioOutput);
```
