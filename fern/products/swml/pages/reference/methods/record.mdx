Record the call audio in the foreground pausing further SWML execution until recording ends. Use this, for example, to record voicemails.
To record calls in the background in a non-blocking fashion, use the [`record_call`](./record_call.mdx)

<ParamField toc={true} path="record" type="object" required={true}>
  An object that accepts the following properties.
</ParamField>


## **Properties**

<ParamField toc={true} path="record.stereo" type="boolean" default="false">
  Whether to record in stereo mode
</ParamField>


<ParamField toc={true} path="record.format" type="string" default="wav">
  Format (`"wav"`, `"mp3"`, or `"mp4"`)
</ParamField>


<ParamField toc={true} path="record.direction" type="string" default="speak">
  Direction of the audio to record: `"speak"` for what party says, `"listen"` for what party hears
</ParamField>


<ParamField toc={true} path="record.terminators" type="string" default="#">
  String of digits that will stop the recording when pressed
</ParamField>


<ParamField toc={true} path="record.beep" type="boolean" default="false">
  Whether to play a beep before recording
</ParamField>


<ParamField toc={true} path="record.input_sensitivity" type="number" default="44.0">
  How sensitive the recording voice activity detector is to background noise. A larger value is more sensitive. Allowed values from `0.0` to `100.0`.
</ParamField>


<ParamField toc={true} path="record.initial_timeout" type="number" default="4.0 seconds">
  How long, in seconds, to wait for speech to start?
</ParamField>


<ParamField toc={true} path="record.end_silence_timeout" type="number" default="5.0 seconds">
  How much silence, in seconds, will end the recording?
</ParamField>


<ParamField toc={true} path="record.max_length" type="number">
  Maximum length of the recording in seconds.
</ParamField>


<ParamField toc={true} path="record.status_url" type="string">
  HTTP or HTTPS URL to deliver record status events.
</ParamField>


## **Variables**

Set by the method:

- **record_url:** (out) the URL of the newly created recording.
- **record_result:** (out) `success` | `failed`.

## **Examples**

### Record some audio and play it back

<CodeBlocks>
<CodeBlock title="YAML">
```yaml
version: 1.0.0
sections:
  main:
    - play:
        url: 'say:Start speaking after the beep. Press hash to end recording.'
    - record:
        end_silence_timeout: 3
        beep: true
    - play:
        url: 'say:Recording ${record_result}. Playing back recording:'
    - play:
        url: '${record_url}'
```
</CodeBlock>
