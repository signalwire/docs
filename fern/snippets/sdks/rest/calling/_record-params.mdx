<ParamField path="audio" type="object" toc={true}>
  Audio recording configuration. Supported keys:

  - `format` — Recording format: `"mp3"` (default) or `"wav"`
  - `stereo` — Record in stereo (default: `false`)
  - `direction` — Which leg to record: `"listen"`, `"speak"`, or `"both"` (default: `"both"`)
  - `beep` — Play a beep before recording (default: `false`)
  - `initial_timeout` — Maximum time in seconds to wait for speech before stopping
  - `end_silence_timeout` — Time in seconds of silence after speech to stop recording
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="control_id" type="string" toc={true}>
  Unique identifier for this record operation, used to control it later.
  If not provided, one is generated automatically.
</ParamField>

<ParamField path="status_url" type="string" toc={true}>
  A URL to receive status update callbacks for the record operation.
</ParamField>
